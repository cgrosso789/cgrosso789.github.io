This is my son Rob.

His business is called Revolution Tree Works, and it operates in the DFW Metroplex in Texas.

Rob is a USMC veteran and has a BS in Business Administration. He learned to climb trees here in New Hampshire, and then "put his passions to work" by training under a former work-associate in Fort Worth to learn the tree business. He has been very busy ever since!

Both the name of his business and the emblem he flies on his flag (seen in the video, below) are derived from the historic Pine Tree Riot, which happened in Weare, NH, in 1772. You can read about it here: Pine Tree Riot of 1772.

(Incidentally, this same historic event was also an inspiration to the Able Ebenezer Brewing Company, whose delicious beer is brewed right here in Southern New Hampshire. If you are interested, you can learn more about their own reasons for being inspired by this story here: Able Ebenezer.)
