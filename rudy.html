Captain Rudy

He was born an expert...

and he could accomplish anything he wanted to.

And what he wanted to do was to help people, be an electrical engineer and drive a big boat.

He had an enormously tender heart, coupled with an attitude and a sharp wit. He was generous and kind, fiercely competitive and passionately loyal. His wife Laura of 36 years was his best friend, and he was loved and respected by everyone who knew him.

I love you and miss you so much, Rudy. There truly was only ever one you. But I'll see you again someday, so until then...party with Jesus and save me a seat.

Music "See you again" by Wiz Kalifa with Charlie Puth
