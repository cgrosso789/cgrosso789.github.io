about me

Hi...I'm Tini.

It's not that I'm very small (anymore), but when I was, my twin brother permanently dubbed me with whatever he blurted out one day; it could have been worse.

I live in a park, of sorts, in beautiful southern New Hampshire. It's full of "critters." Click critter lover and I'll take you for a tour of my backyard park!

I also love fabric. I love to cut it up into little pieces and sew it back together. And then I love to wrap its gloriously-soft, cottony-warmth around me on a cool fall day! Click fabric fanatic and I'll show you a few more samples.

Being a Mom is the best job ever. The second best is developing software. It's a lot like quilting: you have to envision the "big picture," know how to assemble the smallest pieces into bigger units, and then carefully integrate everything together to achieve something beautiful and functional. Click software developer to see the credentials of this newly-minted MSSD graduate from Boston University.
